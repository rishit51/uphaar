Uphaar provides clothes, books, and other gifts to the poor that has a significant impact on the lives of those we serve. Our organization can helps to improve the standard of living for those who lack basic necessities by providing clothing, educational materials, and other useful items. By providing these items for free, we help alleviate the financial burden on those living in poverty, allowing them to redirect their limited resources towards other essential needs. Additionally, our organization helps to improve the mental well-being of those we serve by showing them that they are not alone and others care about their well-being. Overall, Uphaar provides gifts to the poor to help them improve their quality of life and provide a hope for better future.

Our aim

With the service we are providing, we hope to create impact on the lifes of following people

Children

Many children in underprivileged communities lack basic supplies such as School Supplies, Sports Equipment, Computers and Technology, Health and Hygiene Supplies.By providing these materials, Uphaar can help improve the lives of children and give them a better chance for success in life.

Youth

Uphaar help teenagers in various ways by providing them Educational Materials, Technology and Digital Devices, Vocational Training Materials, Health and Wellness Materials, Sports and Recreational Materials.By providing these materials, Uphaar can help teenagers develop new skills, improve their academic performance, and promote their overall health and well-being.

Old age

Uphaar help the elderly in several ways by providing them with materials such as Mobility Aids, Home Safety Equipment, Health and Wellness Materials, Personal Care Items, Social Engagement Materials.By providing these materials, Uphaar can help improve the lives of the elderly and provide them with the support they need to age with dignity and independence.

Person with disability

Uphaar help people with disabilities in several ways by providing them Assistive Devices, Home Adaptation Materials, Educational Materials, Employment Support Materials, Health and Wellness Materials.By providing these materials, Uphaar can help people with disabilities overcome barriers and achieve greater independence, self-sufficiency, and fulfillment in their lives.
